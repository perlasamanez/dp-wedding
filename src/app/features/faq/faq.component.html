Ceremony & Reception Questions
What time should I arrive to the ceremony?

Please plan to arrive at least 15 minutes before the ceremony time. The recommended time to arrive is at 2:30 PM. The ceremony will start promptly at 3:00 PM.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is Formal. Earth tones are strongly recommended. For more information on the colors, please go back to the Home page.

Will the ceremony, cocktail hour, and reception take place indoors or outdoors?

The ceremony and cocktail hour will take place outdoors. The reception will take place indoors.

Can I bring my kids?

Due to guest limit restrictions, we are unable to accommodate children at our wedding. We apologize for any inconvenience and hope you can still join us!

Can I bring a gift to the wedding?

Your presence is present enough, but should you wish to bring a gift to our wedding, please check out our Gift page to learn more about what you could bring.

How do I RSVP?

Check out our RSVP page to reserve your spot for the wedding. The deadline is on January 3, 2025.
